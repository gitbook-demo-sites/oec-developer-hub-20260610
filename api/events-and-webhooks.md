# Events and webhooks

## Public contract

- event names and when they fire
- signature or verification model
- retry behavior
- ordering guarantees or lack thereof
- sample payloads and change policy

## Internal overlay opportunities

Authenticated notes can include event ownership, known incident patterns, and escalation logic without leaking that operational detail into the partner-facing baseline.
